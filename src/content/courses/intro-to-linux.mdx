This short course is a practical first step into Linux command-line work.

## Course Overview

You will complete one guided challenge in a terminal emulator and practice the commands:

- `ls`
- `ls -a`
- `cd`
- `cd ..`
- `cat`

By the end, you should be able to move through directories, reveal hidden entries, and read files to solve a simple flag hunt puzzle.
