When students see debugging as a skill instead of a punishment, they become far more resilient.

## A repeatable process

1. Reproduce the bug.
2. Isolate one variable.
3. Record observations.
4. Explain the fix in plain language.

This routine reduces panic and helps students build transferable habits.
